S.T.E.A.M - Education System

S.T.E.A.M abbreviation

"S" - Science

"T" - Techology

"E" - Engineering

"A" - Arts

"M" - Maths

Uses

learning scientific methodes and applying them in real life.

Encourages Critical and Creative Thinking

Science

problem selection, experimentation (using the Scientific Method)

Technologies

researching (using online libraries, web searches), communication (blogging, videoconferencing, emailing)

Engineering

building or improving a design/model

Arts

writing, communications, poetry, video presentations, creating models

Maths

collecting data, analyzing data outcomes, performing geometry problems

benefits

  • Increases Curiosity
  • Helps in problem solving
  • More participation while learning
  • Sharing Ideas
  • Improves Leadership Quality
  • Promotes logical thinking
  • Critical thinking

Relation between Arts, Mathematics, Science, Technology and Engineering

Functions of Art

  • Non-motivated functions
  • Motivated functions

Non-motivated functions

  1. Basic human instinct for harmony, balance, rhythm
  2. Experience of the mysterious.
  3. Expression of the imagination
  4. Rituals and symbolic functions.

Motivated functions

  1. Communication
  2. Art as entertainment
  3. Art for social causes
  4. Art as a fitness indicator
